Mundo Interconnect

We are a US Based Fiber Optic Educational and Consulting Company. Mundo Interconnect LLC was founded in 2016 to educate, consult, design, and install optical telecommunication systems in the US and Latin America. We have 75 years of combined experience in Fiber Optic and Telecommunication Industry, which includes theoretical, design and hands on expertise. We have business relationships with multiple Global, US and European Fiber Optic Companies. We provide education and hands on training to students, professionals and any interested parties in fiber optic theory and optical components that are commonly used in telecommunication industry.
